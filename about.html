Founder's Note

Establishing a Legacy of Ethics

When I began my journey as a Chartered Accountant, my vision was to build a practice grounded in integrity, expertise, and lasting relationships. Over the years, that vision has evolved into Dahotre and Dahotre Chartered Accountants, a firm committed to delivering value beyond compliance.

With decades of experience across audit, taxation, and advisory, I have come to believe that true financial guidance is not just about numbers—it is about clarity, trust, and enabling better decisions. At our firm, we focus on simplifying complexities and acting as dependable partners in our clients’ journeys. Our approach is rooted in professionalism, ethical standards, and a deep commitment to quality.

As the business environment continues to evolve, we remain dedicated to adapting and strengthening our capabilities—while staying true to the principles that define us.

Our History

Dahotre & Dahotre is a distinguished Chartered Accountancy Firm that has been an integral part of the financial profession for nearly four decades. Founded on the bedrock principles of honesty and dedication, we provide a wide array of services including Audit and Assurance, Information System Audit, and Corporate Taxation to a versatile clientele.

Through economic shifts and technological advances, our commitment has remained unwavering. We believe that maintaining absolute integrity, transparency, and objectivity is the key to delivering high-quality, enduring value to our clients worldwide.

Our Mission

  • To be the financial backbone for our clients through accurate, timely, and compliant services.
  • To deliver client-first, technology-driven financial solutions that simplify compliance and drive growth.
  • To empower businesses with precise insights, strong compliance, and dependable financial support.
